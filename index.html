HERE’S THE LATEST
Nestled in the heart of Rhode Island, our downtown hub pursues a relentless endeavor to assist businesses in digital transformation. From clients seeking a renewed user experience to collaborating with fellow colleagues, understanding the nature of human technological interaction is the heart of our purpose. The Baseline newsletter seeks to capture the beat of the studio, reflecting what inspires our designers and current project engagements with a variety of clients. Each issue uncovers ongoing partnerships with RISD and the greater Providence area, revealing additional tips and tricks to supplement your workflow. With our need for digital connection expanding, we adapt so we can help others succeed in this new world.
